Sri Lankan Traditions

A Kandyan Celebration

❁ ✦ ❁ ✦ ❁

A Kandyan wedding is a sacred tapestry woven from centuries of tradition. The ceremony begins with the Poruwa — a ceremonial platform adorned with white flowers — where the couple stands together as a symbol of their shared future.

The binding of the little fingers with a golden thread, the exchange of gold coins, and the pouring of water over joined hands are ancient rites that bind two souls in love, witnessed by family, community, and the divine.

We celebrate not only the union of Malshi and Sadeep but the coming together of two families, two stories, and one beautiful future.
